💡 BABY TIPS

Tips for Traveling with a 25-Month-Old

Taiwan is extremely family and baby-friendly. Here are some quick tips.

🚇
Strollers on MRT
Taipei MRT has elevators everywhere. It's very convenient to use a stroller. There are also priority sections on the trains.
🍼
Baby Care Rooms
Department stores and public spaces have excellent nursing/diaper changing rooms. Search for 'Baby Care Room' on Google Maps.
🏥
Emergency Medical
Travel insurance is a must. Large hospitals can communicate in English. Always bring essential medicines like fever reducers and band-aids.
🛒
Convenience Store Heaven
7-Eleven and FamilyMart sell everything from diapers to baby snacks. You can easily use their microwaves too.
🚕
Private Cars
When visiting Jiufen/Yehliu, a private KKday car tour is highly recommended. You can request a baby car seat in advance.
👜
Baby Carrier vs Stroller
Stroller for flat areas, carrier for Jiufen/stairs. Make sure to bring both!
